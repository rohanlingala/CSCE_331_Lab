resume

My resume consists of a variety of different hardware and software projects. The types of projects I primarily enjoy are ones where I have to both use Hardware and software, or utilize clever and novel thinking to solve problems. For example, with the Oscilloscope project, I had to create a new and novel way to analyze waveform shape because our Raspberry Pi 4b had trouble notating raw analog values, instead only giving high and low values. Using the ratio of high to low values over a period that was algorithmically determined, we were able to detect waveform shapes given certain frequency and voltage tolerances. This kind of unique thinking is something I would like to bring to the table in terms of creative and clever problem solving.

I also bring a good amount of soft skills to the table, I am able to be a team player or a leader if needed, in many different projects I have shifted from a worker bee to a more managerial role and am able to understand the nuance and dichotomy of working in tandem with others. Feel free to email me about anything if you are interested in looking at the code!
